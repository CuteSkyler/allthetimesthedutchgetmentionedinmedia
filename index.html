House M.D.

One of the supporting characters talks to a computer program providing more data about a current case; coincidentally, the girl on the computer program is a Dutch woman talking in a heavy Dutch accent.

Oppenheimer

Cillian Murphy, the actor playing the role of Oppenheimer, gave a lecture in the Universiteit van Leiden in Dutch about nuclear/atomic physics.

Spider-Man: Far From Home

Friends
